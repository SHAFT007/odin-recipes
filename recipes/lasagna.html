Lasagna Recipe

Description

A classic Italian dish, lasagna is a hearty pasta dish layered with rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and cheese. Perfect for family gatherings or special occasions.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 (28 ounces) can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 2 teaspoons dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions; drain and set aside.
  3. In a skillet, cook ground beef, onion, and garlic over medium heat until meat is browned. Drain excess fat.
  4. Add crushed tomatoes, oregano, salt, and pepper to the skillet. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese, egg, and half of the Parmesan cheese.
  6. Spread a layer of meat sauce in a baking dish. Layer with noodles, ricotta mixture, mozzarella cheese, and more meat sauce. Repeat layers until all ingredients are used, finishing with mozzarella and remaining Parmesan on top.
  7. Bake in preheated oven for 30-35 minutes or until cheese is bubbly and golden brown.
