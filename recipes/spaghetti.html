~Spaghetti recipe~

Description

This is a simple spaghetti recipe with ground beef and tomato sauce. It's a classic, vibrant and flavorful spaghetti sauce dish cooked in a rich sauce with Ground Beef

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 small green bell pepper, diced
  • 1 (28 ounces) can diced tomatoes
  • 1 (16 ounce) can tomato sauce
  • 1 (6 ounce) can tomato paste
  • 2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 2 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • Â½ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Spaghetti pasta
    •

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Combine ground beef, onion, garlic, and green pepper in a large saucepan over medium-high heat. Cook and stir until meat is browned and crumbly and vegetables are tender, 5 to 7 minutes. Drain grease.
  3. Stir in diced tomatoes, tomato sauce, and tomato paste into pan. Season with oregano, basil, salt, and pepper. Simmer spaghetti sauce for 1 hour, stirring occasionally.
  4. Serve hot over cooked spaghetti pasta and enjoy!
